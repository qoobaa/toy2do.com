Hi there! I'm Magdalena, a happy young mum. I've also been working as a teacher, supporting kids with various needs, including visually impaired.

I understand how important it is to provide kids with materials adapted to their age and needs, to stimulate their development in a fun way.

That inspired me to start making toys for babies. My contrastive black/white/red toys help the little ones practise their eyesight, and a variety of textures and sounds stimulate their senses, giving them joy as well! The toys are also suitable for blind and visually impaired children.

In my home section you'll find toys for bigger kids (adults).
